Mclaren Elva

On the left is an all black 2021 Mclaren Elva, ranking among the quickest of cars on the market.

  • MSRP starting at 1,695,000
  • Twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V-8
  • Its 0 to 60 time is 2.6 seconds, and a top speed of 203mph
  • 804 horsepower
  • 590 lb-ft of torque
  • Mclaren's lightest road car ever.



Mclaren Senna GTR

The 2020 Mclaren Senna GTR was crafted to be the direct and responsive car they've ever built, meant to set new benchmarks on the track.

  • MSRP starting at 1,650,000
  • Twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V-8
  • Its 0 to 60 time is 2.8 seconds, and a top speed of 210mph
  • 814 horsepower
  • 590 lb-ft of torque
  • Mclaren's Senna GTR was designed to be a road car meant for the track.

Mclaren Senna

The Mclaren Senna as the fastest lap times of any Mclaren to date.

  • MSRP starting at 1,200,000
  • Twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V-8
  • Its 0 to 62 time is 2.8 seconds, and a top speed of 208mph
  • 814 horsepower
  • 590 lb-ft of torque
  • There are only 75 Mclaren Senna's in the world.

Mclaren Speedtail

The 2020 Mclaren Speedtail is Mclaren's most expensive, powerful, and fast car in the Ultimate Series.

  • MSRP starting at 2,000,000
  • Twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V-8 and an electric motor.
  • Its 0 to 60 time is 2.9 seconds, and a top speed of 250mph
  • 1035 horsepower
  • 848 lb-ft of torque
  • Mclaren's Speedtail is one of their only cars with the drivers seat in the middle.
